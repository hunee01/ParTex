JsPartix

By heirloom hunee. Web visual of the ParTex Project

Made with three.js. 9 or 0 to zoom. RAlt,RCtrl,period,LeftArrow to pan camera L,R,U,D. p,n = proton,neutron; 0,1,2/q,w,e = electron,muon,tau/positron,antimuon,antitau; 4,5/r,t = up,down/antiup,antidown; g = photon; Particles spawn at camera centre.
